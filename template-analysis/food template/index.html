— SINGLE ORIGIN
Beef From One Farm

We work with a single regenerative ranch in upstate New York. One
herd, one feed program, one consistent flavour. Grass-fed, grain-
finished, dry-aged 28 days in our own cooler.

— STONE MILL
Buns From Real Flour

Our brioche starts with stone-milled heritage wheat from a family
farm in Pennsylvania. Slow ferment, butter folded by hand, baked
every morning at 4am — never the day before.

One Patty
Zero Compromise

Ember & Bun started in a small backyard with one cast-iron skillet and an obsession. We wanted to know how good a burger could really be when nothing was outsourced.

Twelve years later, we still grind our own beef every morning, bake our brioche before sunrise, and hand-pickle every cucumber that hits the bun. The flame is always on.
