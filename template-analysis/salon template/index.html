— THE FOUNDER
Twelve Years,
One Intention

Maren trained in Lisbon, refined her practice across boutique houses in
Paris and Copenhagen, and returned home with a single intention — to
build the room she always wished existed: clinically precise, deeply
calm, and never, ever rushed.

Today she leads a small team of CIDESCO and ITEC-certified
estheticians, each chosen as much for their hands as for their hearts.
We are quietly proud to be the spa our own clients send their daughters
and mothers to.

never, ever rushed.”
